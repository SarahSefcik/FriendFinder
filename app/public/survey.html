Personality Test

We’re all blend of different personality types, but we usually have one or two dominant styles. The handiest way of describing them is in terms of four animals: lions, otters, golden retrievers and beavers. Here’s a quick, not very scientific-but-interesting-anyway personality test.

There are no right and wrong answers - tick as many of the traits below that best describe you. Once you’re finished, you will be sorted in to one of the animal personality types and you will be paired with a fellow friend.
